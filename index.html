Delete Your Account & Data

We respect your right to be forgotten. This page explains exactly how to request deletion of your DailyHistory account and all associated data.

⚠️
This action is permanent and irreversible. Once your account and data are deleted, they cannot be recovered. Your reading streak, XP, achievements, and history will be permanently lost.
📱 Method 1 — Delete directly in the App (Recommended)
  • 1
    Open DailyHistory Launch the app on your Android device.
  • 2
    Go to Settings Tap the profile icon or the menu in the top corner, then select Settings.
  • 3
    Open Account Settings Tap Account in the Settings menu.
  • 4
    Tap "Delete Account" Confirm your decision when prompted. Deletion begins immediately.
✉️

Method 2 — Request via Email

If you can't access the app, send us an email from the address linked to your account. We'll delete your account within 30 days of your request.
